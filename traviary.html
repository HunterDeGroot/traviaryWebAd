Traviary is the app for travelers. The user logs their location on their travels and gets points for traveling to different states, countries, territories, and completing various challenges.

The countries tab will show the logged countries a user has been to.

This is a U.S. friendly app. The US tab is dedicated to showing our fellow Americans the states they've been to.

There are several challenges with their descriptions available on-click.

Traviary uses google maps for a great UI customers know and love.
